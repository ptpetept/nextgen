Slide 3: GitHub CoPilot - definitions and takeaways <Review and challenge and suggest improvements>:

- Agent: "a junior who drafts the work, runs the tests, and writes you a memo (the PR) asking for approval."
- Formalise and evolve context - Instructions, skills, requirements, schemas etc
- Human review at every stage of a Research, Plan, Implement & Test loop - not just in the end
- Spec first with end in mind
	Spec should be machine readable and mantainable
- Test (a lot) - both machine and human led testing
- Model choice matters - match capability to the tasks
